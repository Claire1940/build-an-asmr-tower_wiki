<Section icon={Target} title="Sound Block Mechanics and Audio Tier List">

The heart of the building experience lies in the unique acoustic profiles of different materials. Each block type produces a distinct sound when stepped on, ranging from the sharp click of a keyboard to the deep squish of a stress ball. Combining these sounds in a rhythmic sequence is what makes a tower truly satisfying to climb.

Advanced builders categorize blocks by their acoustic feedback and physical properties. Some blocks offer a crisp, cracking sound that mimics real-world sensory videos, while others provide a softer, more cushioned response. Understanding these profiles allows you to design custom audio pathways.

<Callout type="warning" title="Spacing Hazard">
Placing highly sensitive sound blocks too close to each other can cause audio overlapping. Space your blocks at regular intervals to allow each distinct sound effect to finish playing before the next one triggers.
</Callout>
