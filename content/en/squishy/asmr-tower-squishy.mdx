<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Construction Guide">

Building a stable and aesthetically pleasing tower requires more than just clicking. You must account for the "squishiness" of your materials, which can lead to leaning or swaying if not managed correctly. Follow these steps to ensure your first major project is a success.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish a Wide Foundation">
    Start by placing a 3x3 or 5x5 grid of heavy foam blocks. A wider base provides the necessary support for the more elastic blocks you will place later. Avoid using jelly or liquid blocks for the first five floors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Layering Textures">
    As you move upward, alternate between firm and soft textures. This "sandwich" method prevents the tower from becoming too unstable while still providing the satisfying compression that defines the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Incorporate Structural Decorations">
    Use items like "Support Vines" or "Glow Pillars" every ten floors. These aren't just for show; they help lock the blocks in place and reduce the horizontal sway caused by the squishy physics engine.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Aesthetic Polish">
    Once you reach your target height, use the "Soft Brush" tool to adjust the colors and transparency of your tower. Adding a "Mist Aura" at the top can create a beautiful transition into the sky.
  </Step>
</Steps>
