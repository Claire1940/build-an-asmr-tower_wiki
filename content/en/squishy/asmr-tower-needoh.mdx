<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Stacking Strategies for Bouncy Towers">

Stacking squishy materials presents a unique physical challenge. Unlike solid wooden or stone blocks, NeeDoh blocks compress under weight. If you load too much weight onto one side of the tower, the blocks underneath will deform unevenly, causing your entire tower to tilt and eventually collapse.

To prevent this, you must master the art of weight distribution and dampening. Using a mix of different material types can help stabilize your tower. Placing heavier, low-bounce blocks at key structural points will absorb the vibrations of newly dropped blocks, keeping your tower upright even at extreme heights.
