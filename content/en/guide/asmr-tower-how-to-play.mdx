<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Guide">

Navigating the tower is not just about the sounds; it is a test of platforming skill. Follow these steps to ensure you reach the top without falling back to the base levels.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Entry Phase">
    Start by selecting your initial sound preference. Early choices like "Chocolate vs. Honey" are low-stakes. Use this time to get used to the movement physics of your Roblox avatar on these specific block types.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The NPC Encounter">
    Mid-way through the tower, you will encounter NPCs like the "Whisper NPC." These characters provide vocal ASMR. To progress, you must move close to them to trigger the audio cue before the next set of platforms appears.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigating Hazards">
    Be wary of "not cat-proof" sections. These are narrow beams or spinning obstacles where one wrong move sends you falling. Focus on the center of the platform and use the "Shift Lock" feature in Roblox for better camera control.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Ascent">
    The top of the tower often features "Slow Rising" blocks. These platforms move vertically at a slow pace. You must time your jumps so that you land as the block reaches its peak, giving you enough height to reach the next ledge.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Platform Reset">
Some platforms in the ASMR Tower are "one-way" or disappear after a few seconds. If you hear a fading sound effect, it means the platform beneath you is about to vanish. Keep moving!
</Callout>
