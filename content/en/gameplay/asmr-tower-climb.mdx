<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step World Completion Guide">

Navigating the **asmr tower climb** requires a mix of patience and timing. Because the game focuses on satisfying interactions, some platforms may behave differently than they appear (e.g., being extra bouncy or having a delay in the "cracking" animation).

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Setup and Free Rewards">
    Before starting your climb, check the "Daily Logins" and "Free Spins" menus. You can often land a "Skip" or a coin multiplier that makes the first world much faster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mastering the Squishy Jump">
    In World 1, focus on the center of the squishy dumpling platforms. Jumping on the edges can sometimes result in a "glitchy" bounce that sends you off the tower.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigating the Hazard Levels">
    Once you reach the magenta and gray levels in later worlds, watch out for lasers and lava. These are "one-hit" kills that will send you back to the last checkpoint.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Ascent">
    World 3 features "bouncy pops" that are notoriously difficult. Wait for the platform to fully reset its animation before making your next move to avoid losing momentum.
  </Step>
</Steps>
