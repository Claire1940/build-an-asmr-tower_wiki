<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Keyboard Switch Profiles & Comparison">

At the heart of the tower's sound design is the simulation of various mechanical keyboard switches. Mechanical keyboard enthusiasts will immediately recognize the distinct characteristics of tactile, clicky, and linear switches. The game translates these physical hardware sensations into digital audio files that trigger with every step your character takes. Understanding these profiles can help you choose the paths that deliver the highest level of sensory satisfaction.

Tactile switches provide a noticeable bump in their sound profile, resulting in a chunky, rounded noise that feels incredibly solid. Clicky switches, on the other hand, produce a sharp, metallic snap that is highly defined and high-pitched. Linear switches offer a smoother, quieter glide, which is ideal for players who find high-frequency clicks distracting during difficult platforming segments. Optic switches bridge the gap, offering a rapid-fire, lightweight click that responds instantly to your movement speed.
